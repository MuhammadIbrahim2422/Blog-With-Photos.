This is the Solar System.

This is the Sun. The Sun is in the middle of the Solar System. The Sun is a star.

This is Mercury. Mercury is the closest planet to the sun.

This is Venus. Venus is the hottest planet in the Solar System.

This is Earth. Earth is the planet that humans live on.

This is Mars. Mars is the 4th planet from the sun.

This is Jupiter. Jupiter is the largest planet in the Solar System.

This is Saturn. Saturn has 500 to 1000 rings made by small chunks of ice, rocks, and comets.

This is Uranus. Uranus is made of water, methane, and ammonia fluids above a small rocky center.

This is Neptune. Neptune is the eighth and last planet from the sun.
